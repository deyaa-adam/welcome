About Us

IEEE, Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, is the world’s largest non-profit technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.The Hashemite University Student Branch is considered part of the Jordan Section, which is a part of Region 8. The Hashemite University Student Branch, which was established in 2002, is one of the best student branches in the region.

IEEE includes 39 technical societies besides several affinity groups and committees. One of these societies is the EMBS (The Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society). It is the world’s largest international society of biomedical engineers.

IEEE EMBS HU Student Chapter (Engineering in Medicine and Biology Society) is the first chapter of EMBS that was created in the IEEE Jordan Section and the first technical society chapter included in IEEE HU Student Branch at the beginning of 2012.

  • Best New Student Branch in IEEE 2014-2013.
  • EMBS Regional Outstanding Student Chapter/ Club 2019-2018.

The chapter produce significant efforts to provide students with practical experience, a closer view of the work environment, and a thorough understanding of the basics of biomedical engineering.

Enrollment And Membership

Anyone can enroll in IEEE EMBS at the Hashemite University through one of the following methods:

Officer

Enrollment in the IEEE student branch is annual and is done by filling out a registration form that is published on social media sites at the end of each year.

Volunteer

You can volunteer in one of our large events when we request volunteers, and this is for one event only, by filling out the registration form if it comes out, or contacting the officials directly.

Membership

The membership is open to all. Anyone can join the EMBS membership by obtaining the basic membership of the IEEE and then registering it in the membership of the Society of Engineering in Medicine and Biology. Membership subscription is annual and you can renew it continuously.

Membership subscription or renewal is through direct registration from the main IEEE website or through the student branch office at the Hashemite University in the Facility of Engineering, Department of Electrical Engineering, second floor.

News And Publications

IEEE EMBS HU chapter regularly updates their members and subscribers with the latest news related to biomedical engineering, healthcare technology, and other relevant fields. The news usually covers various topics such as research updates, new technologies, industry developments, and upcoming events organized by the chapter.In addition, the global EMBS publishes various materials related to biomedical engineering and healthcare technology, including conference proceedings, journals, and magazines. The publications cover a wide range of topics such as biomedical imaging, neural engineering, clinical engineering, and many more.
